**SQ** Slaver, Sneak Attack

---
**Special Qualities**

+ Slaver

Kitsune gains +2 to all non-lethal damage, and does not suffer from usual -4 penalty to attack when dealing non-lethal damage with lethal weapons. They also gain +4 to grapple to downed targets, and always succeed forcefully relocating grappled targets.

+ Sneak Attack

Kitsune Rogues add 1d4 damage to attacks against targets who are flanked, out-maneuvered or flat footed.
